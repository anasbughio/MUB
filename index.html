Welcome to MUB Trust Hospital

Located in the heart of Mirpurkhas, MUB Trust Hospital is committed to providing quality healthcare services to the people of the region. Established with the aim of serving the underprivileged, MUB Trust Hospital has grown to become a symbol of hope and care. Our mission is to deliver accessible and affordable healthcare to all, regardless of race, color, or socioeconomic background. With a focus on patient-centered care, MUB Trust Hospital offers a wide range of medical services, including outpatient, inpatient, and specialized clinics, in a state-of-the-art facility. We are dedicated to improving the health and well-being of the community through compassionate care, modern treatments, and advanced medical technology. Our hospital is more than just a healthcare facility—it is a place where the community finds support, comfort, and trust. At MUB Trust Hospital, we are committed to expanding our services to meet the growing needs of our patients while upholding the highest standards of medical excellence.

VISION & MISSION

Vision

"To provide exceptional healthcare services to all, regardless of economic status, and to deliver excellence in medical education and training for future healthcare professionals."

Mission

“To ensure access to high-quality healthcare for the underprivileged by offering comprehensive, patient-centered medical services. We are committed to providing free healthcare without discrimination of caste, color, or creed. Our goal is to foster a culture of respect, dignity, and compassion for all patients. Through state-of-the-art clinical practices, cutting-edge research, and ethical medical training, we aim to develop the next generation of medical professionals. We believe in empowering communities by promoting disease prevention and raising public health awareness, while opposing the commercialization of healthcare.”

Donation In kind

In Pakistan, almost 78% of the population lacks access to affordable medical care. At MUB Trust Hospital, we take this as our mission to step in and make a difference. Every day, we are dedicated to helping those who can't afford essential medical treatment, providing hope and healing to thousands of patients.
Our vision is clear: to continuously improve the quality of healthcare services we provide.
