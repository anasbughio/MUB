ENT

At MUB Hospital, the Ear, Nose, and Throat (ENT) Department is committed to providing expert care for a wide range of ENT disorders. Led by Prof. Shahid Hussain, our team offers both conventional and super-specialized treatments, ensuring high-quality, affordable healthcare for all, particularly the underserved communities.

Key Services:

  • Micro-Ear Surgeries: Utilizing state-of-the-art operating microscopes, we specialize in micro-ear surgeries that allow for precision and minimal impact on surrounding tissues. These procedures are critical for treating ear infections, hearing loss, and related conditions.
  • Endoscopic Sinus Surgery: Our department is equipped with the latest endoscopic technology, allowing for minimally invasive sinus surgeries that ensure quicker recovery and less discomfort. We prioritize preserving normal structures while effectively treating sinus issues such as chronic sinusitis and nasal polyps.

Commitment to Accessibility:

At MUB Hospital, we believe in providing affordable healthcare without compromising on quality. We offer discounted rates for complex and super-specialized procedures to make advanced treatments accessible to those in need, especially the poor and deserving patients in our community.

Vision:

Our vision is to combine innovative surgical techniques with compassionate care to help our patients achieve the best possible outcomes. Whether managing a chronic condition or providing one-time surgical intervention, the ENT Department at MUB Hospital is here to support you at every step.
