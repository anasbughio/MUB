Plastic Surgery

Welcome to the Plastic & Reconstructive Surgery Department at MUB Hospital, where change and renewal meet compassionate care. Our department specializes in addressing body and facial defects caused by burns, trauma, birth disorders, and various other conditions. Our highly skilled surgeons are dedicated to helping patients regain both their physical appearance and confidence.

Expert Care for Complex Conditions:

  • Burns and Trauma: We provide comprehensive treatment for severe burns and trauma-related injuries. Our specialized care focuses on wound healing, scar revision, and reconstruction, ensuring the best possible outcomes for our patients.
  • Birth Disorders: We address congenital conditions such as cleft lips and palates, providing life-changing corrective surgeries for children and adults. These procedures aim to restore both function and appearance, improving the quality of life for our patients.
  • Microvascular Tissue Transplantation: For complex cases, our team is skilled in microvascular tissue transplantation, using advanced techniques to rebuild damaged or missing tissues with precision and care.

Our Commitment to Patient-Centered Care:

At MUB Hospital, we understand the emotional and physical impact of body and facial defects. Our team is committed to providing compassionate, personalized care tailored to each patient’s unique needs. From the initial consultation to post-surgery recovery, we stand by our patients every step of the way.

Vision:

Our goal is to deliver world-class plastic and reconstructive surgery services, using the most advanced techniques while maintaining a deep commitment to the well-being of our patients. We’re here to restore confidence, function, and a renewed sense of self.
