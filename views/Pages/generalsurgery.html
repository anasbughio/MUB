General Surgery

At MUB Hospital, our General Surgery Department is committed to delivering high-quality surgical care to all our patients. With a team of highly experienced surgeons and professors, our department offers comprehensive services for a variety of surgical conditions, ensuring that each patient receives personalized and compassionate care.

Facilities and Capacity:

  • Two Surgery Units: We have two fully equipped units, each with 90 beds and a state-of-the-art Intensive Care Unit (ICU). Our facilities are designed to handle both emergency and elective surgeries 24/7, ensuring patients receive the critical care they need at any time.
  • Recognized Training Center: Our department is recognized by the College of Physicians and Surgeons of Pakistan (CPSP) for training the next generation of surgeons. We take pride in fostering medical education and contributing to the future of surgical excellence.

Our Key Services:

  • Abdominal and Gastrointestinal Surgery: Our team of experts specializes in surgeries for gastrointestinal conditions such as hernias, appendicitis, gallbladder disease, and colorectal disorders.
  • Breast Surgery: We offer specialized surgical procedures for breast conditions, including lumpectomy, mastectomy, and reconstructive surgeries, ensuring comprehensive care for both benign and malignant conditions.
  • Hernia Repair: Utilizing the latest minimally invasive techniques, our department is experienced in hernia repair, offering faster recovery and less postoperative discomfort.

Patient-Centered Care:

We understand that surgery can be an overwhelming experience. At MUB Hospital, we are committed to making the process as smooth and reassuring as possible. Our surgeons and medical staff work closely with each patient to develop a personalized treatment plan that aligns with their specific needs and goals. From diagnosis through recovery, we provide ongoing support and compassionate care.

A Holistic Approach:

At MUB Hospital, we don’t just treat the surgical condition – we care for the whole patient. Our multidisciplinary approach ensures that our patients receive comprehensive care by collaborating with specialists from other departments, including gastroenterology, oncology, and radiology.
