Gynocologist & Obstetrics

At MUB Trust Hospital, the Gynaecology & Obstetrics Department is dedicated to providing exceptional care for women at all stages of life. Our experienced team of Obstetricians and Gynecologists, supported by advanced technology and modern facilities, is committed to ensuring the highest standards of care for our patients.

Our Services Include:

  • Normal Delivery & C-Section: Expert care for both routine and high-risk deliveries, ensuring a safe and comfortable experience for both mother and baby.
  • D&C (Dilation and Curettage): Providing safe and effective treatment for various gynecological conditions and post-miscarriage care.
  • Laparotomy & Hysterectomy: Advanced surgical procedures to address a range of gynecological issues, including fibroids, endometriosis, and other conditions requiring surgical intervention.
  • Fistula Treatment: Comprehensive management and repair of obstetric and gynecological fistulas. Infertility Clinic: Specialized care for couples facing challenges with conception, including diagnostic evaluation and treatment options to support fertility.

Infrastructure Highlights:

  • Well-Equipped Labor Room: Featuring ergonomic birthing chairs to enhance comfort during labor. Continuous Fetal Monitoring: State-of-the-art technology to ensure the well-being of both mother and baby throughout labor.
  • Day Care Facilities: For various procedures and surgeries, providing convenience and comfort. Modular Operation Theatres: Equipped with advanced laparoscopic equipment to perform minimally invasive surgeries.
  • NICU & ICU Support: A 6-bed Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) supported by an Intensive Care Unit (ICU) for complex deliveries and newborn care.

Specialized Services:

  • Preconception Counselling: Guidance and planning to optimize health before pregnancy.
  • Pregnancy Management: Care for pregnancies with structural fetal defects, genetic disorders, medical complications, infectious diseases, and multiple pregnancies.
  • Gynecological Problem Management: Expertise in handling a wide range of issues including sub-fertility, recurrent miscarriage, and various gynecological conditions.
  • Advanced Endoscopic & Laparoscopic Surgery: For conditions such as hysterectomy, ovarian cysts, endometriosis, fibroids, and more.
  • Family Planning: Comprehensive services to support reproductive health and family planning goals.

Fetal Medicine Unit:

  • First Trimester Screening: For conditions such as Down's Syndrome.
  • Genetic Sonogram & Amniocentesis: For assessing genetic conditions including thalassemia and cystic fibrosis.
  • Preterm Delivery Screening: Evaluation and management to reduce risks associated with preterm birth.
  • Fetal Growth Restriction Evaluation: Monitoring and managing growth restrictions and twin pregnancies.
  • Counselling for Fetal Abnormalities: Support and guidance for families facing fetal abnormalities.

At MUB Trust Hospital, our mission is to provide compassionate, high-quality care to women, ensuring their health and well-being at every stage of life.
