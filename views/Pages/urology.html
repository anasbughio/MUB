Urology

Welcome to the Urology Department at MUB Hospital, where our mission is to provide comprehensive care for all urological conditions. Established with a commitment to excellence, we are equipped with the latest technology and a dedicated team of expert urologists and surgeons. Our department specializes in treating a variety of urological disorders such as kidney stones, prostate issues, urinary incontinence, and cancers of the urinary tract. We offer both open and minimally invasive surgical procedures, ensuring patient comfort and faster recovery.

Some of the advanced technologies we use include:

  • Flexible Ureterorenoscopes for minimally invasive kidney stone removal.
  • Pediatric Cystoscopes for child-friendly diagnostics and treatment.
  • Resectoscopes for precision prostate and bladder procedures.

At MUB Hospital, our Urology Department is at the cutting edge of medical advancements, providing personalized and holistic care for patients with urological disorders. We understand that urological issues can be sensitive and life-altering, which is why we focus on delivering care with empathy, privacy, and precision.

Our Key Services Include:
  • Kidney Stone Management: Utilizing state-of-the-art technology such as Extracorporeal Shock Wave Lithotripsy (ESWL) and Laser Lithotripsy, we offer non-invasive and minimally invasive procedures to break down kidney stones efficiently.
  • Prostate Health: From benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH) to prostate cancer, our specialists use advanced diagnostic tools like PSA screening, MRI fusion biopsies, and transurethral resection of the prostate (TURP) to ensure early detection and optimal treatment.
  • Bladder and Urinary Incontinence Treatments: We offer a range of treatments for overactive bladder, stress incontinence, and other urinary disorders. Our team is skilled in both conservative management and surgical options like sling procedures and sacral nerve modulation.
  • Pediatric Urology: We specialize in treating congenital and acquired urological conditions in children, such as vesicoureteral reflux (VUR), hypospadias, and undescended testicles. Our pediatric urologists ensure gentle and effective care for our young patients.

At MUB Hospital, we are committed to enhancing your health with compassionate and high-quality urological care
