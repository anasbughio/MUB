Chairman Message

Alhamdulillah, by the grace of Allah and with the unwavering dedication of our team and supporters, MUB Trust Hospital has made significant strides in delivering quality healthcare to the people of Mirpurkhas. Our hospital, which started as a small initiative, has rapidly grown to meet the increasing healthcare needs of the community. Under the leadership of Dr. Tariq Umer, MUB Trust Hospital has become a center of excellence in providing free, accessible healthcare to all. Our modern facilities, including advanced diagnostic services and specialized medical departments, are equipped to handle the growing patient volume. We are committed to ensuring that the people of Mirpurkhas and surrounding areas receive the care they need, regardless of their financial background. Our mission is inspired by leading healthcare models in Pakistan, and with continued support from donors and philanthropists, we aim to expand our services to reach even more patients. Recently, we have added more beds and expanded our surgical departments to cope with the increasing demand, ensuring that we can serve even more people in the years to come, InshaAllah. Your trust and support are the driving forces behind our success. Together, we can continue to build a healthier future for our community. Allah Hafiz!

Dr. Tariq Umer
